Django AB-Testing

What is this lib about?

AB Testing is very specific, but on higher level it becomes always the same: you prepare two ore more experiments to measure the success.

The lib takes care for managing tests, goals, results and experiments. 'Managing' means providing the models; together with appropriated helper, giving you always access to current experiment and goals.
